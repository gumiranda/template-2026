

# Restaurant Digital Menu Landing Page

A modern, conversion-focused landing page to persuade restaurants to adopt your digital menu and QR code solution. Design follows the clean, professional aesthetic of the reference with a green and white color scheme.

---

## 1. Header with Navigation
- **Sticky navigation bar** that remains visible while scrolling
- Logo on the left with clear brand identity
- Navigation links: Features, How It Works, Pricing, About
- **Search functionality** for quick information lookup
- Login and "Get Started" buttons on the right
- **Fully responsive** - collapses to hamburger menu on mobile devices

---

## 2. Hero Section
- **Bold headline:** "Transform Your Restaurant with Digital Menus"
- Compelling subheadline explaining the value proposition
- Two prominent CTAs: "Get Started" and "View Demo"
- **Device mockups** showing the digital menu in action (phone/tablet)
- Trusted indicator: "Trusted by 500+ restaurants"
- **Trusted brands logo bar** showcasing well-known restaurant brands
- Subtle background animation for visual appeal

---

## 3. Statistics Section
- **Animated counters** that count up when scrolled into view
- 4 impressive metrics:
  - 30% reduction in wait times
  - 500+ restaurants using the platform
  - 25% increase in average order value
  - 98% customer satisfaction rate
- Clean icons accompanying each statistic
- Brief context explaining the impact

---

## 4. How It Works (3 Steps)
- **Step 1: Create Your Menu** - Upload dishes, photos, and prices through the intuitive platform
- **Step 2: Generate QR Codes** - Get customized QR codes for each table
- **Step 3: Receive Orders** - Orders flow directly to the kitchen in real-time
- Visual icons/illustrations for each step
- Numbered badges with connecting flow

---

## 5. Features Section
- **Feature cards with images** showing the solution in action:
  - **Complete White-Label Solution** - Custom branding for your restaurant
  - **Kitchen Display Dashboard** - Real-time order management for staff
  - **Multi-Cart System** - Handle dine-in and delivery simultaneously
- "View all features" link for more details
- Clean card layout with hover effects

---

## 6. Testimonials Section
- **"What Our Customers Say"** heading
- 3 testimonial cards with:
  - Customer quote
  - Photo, name, and role (restaurant owner/manager)
  - Restaurant name
- Realistic, authentic-sounding quotes highlighting specific benefits
- Star ratings for credibility

## 7. Pricing Section
- **Toggle between Monthly/Annual** billing (Annual shows discount)
- 3 pricing tiers:
  - **Starter (Free)**: Perfect for small cafes, basic features
  - **Pro ($89/mo)**: For growing restaurants, full features - *Most Popular*
  - **Enterprise ($199/mo)**: Franchises, unlimited locations, API access
- Clear feature lists for each tier
- Prominent CTA buttons

---

## 8. Final CTA Section
- **Dark background** with compelling headline: "Ready to Modernize Your Restaurant?"
- Subtext about joining 500+ restaurants
- Two buttons: "Create Free Account" and "Schedule Demo"

## 9. Footer
- Logo and brief description
- Link columns: Product, Company, Legal
- Social media icons
- Copyright and system status indicator

## Design & Technical Details
- **Color scheme:** Green (#22c55e) as primary, white backgrounds, dark text
- **Smooth scroll animations** with fade-in effects on scroll
- **Fully responsive** for mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Optimized images** with modern formatting
- Clean typography with excellent readability
